Information about Blood Donation

Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation saves lives. Your contribution can help patients in need of blood transfusions.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be at least 18 years old
  • Must weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs)
  • Be in good health and feeling well

How to Prepare

  • Drink plenty of water before your appointment
  • Eat a healthy meal before donating
  • Bring a valid ID
